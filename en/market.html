Analysis of the global collector car market and investment opportunities

Overview

The Bespoke Car (thebespokecar.com) is an investment-grade automotive concierge serving collectors and demanding buyers in France, Italy and across Europe. The firm offers acquisition mandates (sourcing, selection, due diligence, negotiation), sale mandates (premium presentation, buyer targeting), and full import logistics from Japan, Korea, North America and Europe.

Alexandre positions this service as reducing information asymmetry in high-value acquisitions, documenting technical risks and securing execution end-to-end.

The collector car market is booming. Global auction and online sales reached $4.8 billion in 2025, up 10% year-on-year. The global vintage car market is valued at $29.6 billion (2025) and projected to reach $52.8 billion by 2034, with a CAGR of 6.6%. The European classic car market in particular is gaining strong momentum.

Japanese classics are the hottest segment in the market. Models like the Nissan Skyline GT-R and Honda NSX have seen 3x to 5x value appreciation between 2019 and 2025. Younger collectors (Generation X and Millennials) are driving demand for 1990s–2000s performance icons. Hagerty's 2026 Bull Market List strongly favours exactly this era.
