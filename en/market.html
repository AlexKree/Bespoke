Idea Overview

The Bespoke Car (thebespokecar.com) is an investment-grade collector car concierge serving collectors and demanding buyers across France, Italy, and Europe. The business provides buy-side mandates (sourcing, selection, due diligence, negotiation), sell-side mandates (premium presentation, buyer targeting), and full import logistics from Japan, Korea, North America, and Europe. Alexandre positions this as reducing information asymmetry in high-value acquisitions, documenting technical risks, and securing execution end-to-end.

Why the Timing Is Perfect
Multiple structural tailwinds are converging right now.

25-Year Eligibility Wave Unlocks Iconic Models
The 25-year import rule opens floodgates for 2001 production year models — including the Honda S2000 AP1 and Mazda RX-7 Spirit R. Analysts project 30%+ appreciation on first-wave eligible cars.
Honda S2000 AP1  |  Mazda RX-7 Spirit R

EU–Japan Free Trade Agreement
Tariff elimination reduces import costs significantly, making Japanese classics more accessible to European collectors while boosting margins.
✅
Superior Condition from Japan
Cars maintained to higher standards: 6,000 km service intervals, minimal rust (no road salt), and lower average mileage (6–8,000 km/year).
📈
Market Shift Toward Modern Classics
The market is shifting from pre-1970s classics toward 1990s–2000s icons — exactly the era where Japanese cars dominate the collector landscape.
👥
New Generation of Collectors
Gen X and Millennials are entering the collector market with strong demand for the cars of their youth — Skylines, Supras, NSXs and RX-7s.
