Services

Method, diligence, execution.

High-end car transactions depend on file quality: provenance, real condition, preparation costs, liquidity, and disciplined execution. Our approach is structured and documented.

Mission

Bring the rigor of investment banking to the world of collector cars, so every acquisition is a decision made with clarity, not hope.

What we're building

An investment-grade car advisory for collectors and demanding buyers across Europe. We source, inspect, underwrite, and deliver exceptional automobiles from Japan, Europe, and beyond, treating every transaction with the due diligence and structured execution that serious assets deserve.

Where we're headed

A world where acquiring a collector car feels like closing a deal with a trusted principal, not rolling the dice with a stranger across borders. Where every vehicle in a collection has a provenance file as complete as a financial audit. Where the Côte d'Azur isn't just where the cars end up, but where the standard was set for how they're acquired. The Bespoke Car becomes the name collectors trust when the stakes are real and the margin for error is zero.

End-to-end process

From investment thesis to delivery

1 — Brief

Budget, model, criteria, horizon, usage, import constraints.

2 — Sourcing

Seller network, brokers, auctions, off-market opportunities.

3 — Due diligence

PPI/inspection, history, consistency checks, preparation cost estimate.

4 — Negotiation

Pricing strategy, conditions, payment security, documentation.

5 — Import / logistics

Transport, paperwork, reception, storage when required.

6 — Delivery

Handover, final file, maintenance recommendations.
